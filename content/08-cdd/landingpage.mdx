Customer Due Diligence has become a foundation of modern business. At the same time, CDD processes nowadays are so seamless we don’t even notice them.

With CDD on Polymesh there is no difference between off- and on-chain. Polymesh leverages an ecosystem including professional KYC companies to provide quick and easy onboarding for users.

How does CDD work on Polymesh? Let’s explore the main characteristics and concepts, and round up everything with an example scenario using the Dashboard and SDK for implementation

<h3>CDD with Polymesh</h3>
Providing CDD on-chain brings efficiency gains and allows to reflect and implement business logics on a blockchain network.

Polymesh fulfils CDD as a result of its design fit for markets. CDD claims are made possible with Polymesh’s Polymath Unique Identity System. Additionally, Polymesh brings access to a network of CDD providers ensuring a compliant business platform.

Through an example scenario, we will dive further into CDD on Polymesh. Using the Dashboard and the SDK, we will go through the scenario and explore the possibilities related to CDD.
